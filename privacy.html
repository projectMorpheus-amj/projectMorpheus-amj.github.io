  6. Your Rights

    • Access: You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you.
    • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate information.

  7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the app. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.
